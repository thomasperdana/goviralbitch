### Key Takeaways for Charles

1. **Comment-gating is the #1 engagement hack.** Cooper and Noe both use "comment X and I'll send it" — drives thousands of comments which games the algorithm. Charles should build comment-gatable Claude skills.

2. **"Replace your [expensive service] with this free tool" is a proven template.** Cooper ran it twice (#1 SEO, #6 ads) with near-identical structure and both crushed. This is a repeatable format Charles can use for: accounting, legal intake, project management, etc.

3. **Noe's vulnerability reel (#4) got the highest like ratio (18K likes, only 1.5K comments).** Emotional/relatable content drives likes and shares. No tutorial needed — just be real.

4. **News-jacking works when you're FAST.** Both Cooper and Noe posted about OpenClaw dying on the same day. First-mover advantage on AI news = engagement.

5. **Noe is literally building Charles's content pipeline.** Reel #5 describes the exact same system (researcher → ideator → scripter → analyst). Charles should be the one showing this — he has the same system and MORE depth.

6. **Speed > depth for Reels.** Most of these are 15-40 seconds. The shorter the reel, the higher the completion rate. #2 (Noe's Meta ads) is under 30 seconds and got 31K engagement.

7. **Neither James Goldbach nor Sam Gaudet cracked the top 10.** Cooper and Noe are the real competition on IG. Consider whether James and Sam are still relevant competitors or need replacement.
